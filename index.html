Chebarbar Pizza

The Best Charcoal Grilled Pizza in Eldoret Town!


Chebarbar Pizza is a family business located in Eldoret, Kenya. We offer the best homemade charcoal grilled pizza in town, made with the highest quality locally produced ingredients.

The homemade pizza is produced in-house daily and made according to Neopolitan tradition. We use only the best quality ingredients which are grown in our home gardn such as tomatoes, onions, dhania,hoho,pepper, homemade mozzarella cheese and drizzled with Italian virgin olive oil and baked to perfection.
