The Best Charcoal Grilled Pizza in Eldoret Town!


Chebarbar Pizza is a family business located in Eldoret, Kenya. We offer the best homemade charcoal grilled pizza in town, made with the highest quality locally produced ingredients.

The homemade pizza is produced in-house daily and made according to Neopolitan tradition. We use only the best quality ingredients such as tomatoes, onions, dhania,hoho,pepper,which are grown in our home garden. We also use homemade mozzarella cheese and drizzle with Italian virgin olive oil and baked to perfection in our fine charcoal wood brick oven.
